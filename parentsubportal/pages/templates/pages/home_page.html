Welcome!

Parent Suportal is a community-tech ‘one-stop shop’ for parents of children with disability and special needs.

Introduction

We are creating an equal playing field for parents and carers of children with disability and additional needs.
Join our movement.
Plan ahead, succeed now, know your options!

View details »

Our aim?

To live in a world where families are adequately supported.
Where resource is fairly distributed and simple barriers are removed, so that parents of children with special needs and disability can be parents first and advocates second.

View details »

Why us?

The online platform consists of a directory of information covering everything you need to know, including a customisable timeline to help plan your child’s first 25 years; an online community (think Mumsnet for disability); and a marketplace of curated products.
